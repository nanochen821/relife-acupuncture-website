About Connie

Traditional knowledge with attentive, personal care

Originally from Taiwan, Connie developed deep roots in traditional Chinese medicine and continued her clinical education through advanced study and hands-on training.

Her professional background includes training in Chinese herbal medicine at medical and traditional Chinese medicine institutions in Nanjing, China.

Throughout her career, she has continued learning from practitioners and professors across Taiwan, China, Canada, and the United States.

Today, Connie maintains her private practice at ReLife Acupuncture, serving patients in Fremont and Pleasanton.

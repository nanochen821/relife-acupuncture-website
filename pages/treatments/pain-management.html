Advanced Pain Management

Acupuncture care for pain, nerve, joint, and mobility concerns

ReLife provides individualized care for a range of acute and chronic pain concerns. Treatment recommendations depend on each patient’s symptoms, health history, and individual assessment.

Areas of Care

Pain concerns currently listed by ReLife

The following categories summarize pain and mobility concerns currently published by the clinic. Contact ReLife to discuss your individual situation.

01

Nerve-Related Pain

Care for pain, numbness, discomfort, or mobility concerns associated with nerve-related conditions.

  • Sciatica
  • Peripheral neuropathy
  • Nerve and limb discomfort
  • Pain affecting walking or movement
Ask About Nerve-Related Pain

02

Head, Jaw, Neck & Shoulder

Individualized care for recurring discomfort involving the head, jaw, neck, and shoulders.

  • Migraines and headaches
  • TMJ syndrome
  • Neck pain and stiffness
  • Shoulder and upper-back pain
Ask About Head, Jaw or Neck Pain

03

Back, Hip, Foot & Ankle

Care for pain and restricted movement involving the back, hips, legs, feet, or ankles.

  • Back and lower-back pain
  • Hip and leg discomfort
  • Foot and ankle pain
  • Plantar fasciitis
Ask About Back or Lower-Body Pain

04

Joint, Tendon & Mobility Concerns

Supportive care for joint discomfort, restricted movement, and selected inflammatory or tendon-related concerns.

  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Tennis and golf elbow
  • Trigger finger
  • De Quervain’s disease
  • Ankylosing spondylitis
Ask About Joint or Tendon Pain

A condition appearing on this page does not mean that acupuncture is appropriate for every person. Individual responses vary, and no particular outcome can be guaranteed.

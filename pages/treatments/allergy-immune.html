Before Your Appointment

Information that may help your consultation

Be prepared to describe when symptoms occur, what seems to make them better or worse, and any care you are already receiving.

01

Symptom Patterns

Note when your symptoms appear

Consider whether symptoms change with seasons, locations, foods, products, stress, sleep, or other circumstances.

02

Existing Care

Share relevant health information

Tell the clinic about relevant diagnoses, allergy testing, prescribed medications, and care from other healthcare providers.

Seek urgent medical care for severe breathing difficulty, facial or throat swelling, fainting, or another possible medical emergency.
